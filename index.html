Garlic Bhel

Ingredients

  • 3 cups Puffed rice
  • 5 garlic cloves
  • ¼ cup coconut
  • 3 green chilles
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • 3 tbsp Boondi
  • 3 tbsp Sev
  • 3 tbsp carrot
  • 3 tbsp Coriander
  • 2 tsp sugar powder (optional)

Method

  • Place coconut, green chilli and garlic along with salt in a mixer grinder. Grind it as smooth as possible. Do not add water.
  • Now in a mixing bowl, take puffed rice. Add ghee to it and mix well to coat.
  • Add ground paste, kara boondi, sev and grated carrot, Finely chopped coriander leaves. Add sugar powder at this stage if you are adding.
  • Mix well and your Garlic bhel is ready to eat.
